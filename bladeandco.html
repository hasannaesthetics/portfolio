Our Story

Where craft
meets character.

Blade & Co. was born from a simple belief: a great haircut changes how you carry yourself. We opened our doors in Singapore in 2019, committed to delivering the kind of barbershop experience that's become rare — unhurried, skilled, and personal.

Every barber on our team has been trained in classic techniques, blended with modern styles. We don't rush. We listen. And we don't stop until it's right.
